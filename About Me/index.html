About Me

Hi There! My name is Sushmita Shukla, a 19-year-old freelance model, my journey started in the lockdown period when I started taking interest in modelling and pageants, that was the time when I decided to completely revamp my lifestyle, later on I joined Conquer with Nikita Tanwani which was my first exposure in the pageant industry as well as in front of camera and that is the same place where I got my first portfolio shoot done, there I won the mock pageant as well. In 2023, I decided to join The Cocoaberry Academy where I was supervised under Alesia and Anjli Raut, who have an experience of 18 years in the modelling and pageant industry. In the month of May, I was offered a commercial for an oral care brand, which goes by the name The Brush Bud. I am looking forward to more opportunities in this industry.
